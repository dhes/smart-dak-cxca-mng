# CXCA.D2 — Cervical Cancer Screening Eligibility

<!-- DAK_API_PLACEHOLDER: StructureDefinition-CXCA.D2 -->

Logical model for the inputs and output of the cervical cancer screening eligibility decision (D2). Paired with the QCXCAD2 Questionnaire for data capture and consumed by the CXCAD2DTScreeningEligibilityLogic CQL library. Reflects the binding eligibility rules in Mongolia MOH Order A/641 (2020) §2.2.

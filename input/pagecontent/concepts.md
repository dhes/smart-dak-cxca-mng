Key clinical concepts referenced across this DAK are defined in the [`CXCA.D` CodeSystem](CodeSystem-CXCA.D.html). For the methodology vocabulary distinguishing legal, programmatic, and operational regulatory positions, see the [`CXCA.Bindingness` CodeSystem](CodeSystem-CXCA.Bindingness.html).

The DAK uses standard FHIR primitives and conventions throughout (R4); no cxca-specific glossary or abbreviations file is maintained beyond the CodeSystem displays and definitions. For a brief abbreviation list (CIN, HPV, ASC-US, HSIL, LSIL, NILM, LTFU, etc.), the [`CXCA.D` CodeSystem](CodeSystem-CXCA.D.html) entries provide expansion and clinical context.

Mongolia-specific abbreviations from the source guideline (УХХХ for cervical cancer, ХПВ for HPV, the ЭИМ-5 form codes, etc.) are not currently rendered as DAK concepts but are documented in the methodology overview at [Adapting Guidelines for Country Use](adapting.html).
